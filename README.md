# Radian

Radian is a versatile app designed for music lovers in India. It allows users to stream songs from online sources, download live songs, and play all India radio stations. With a focus on India's diverse radio stations, Radian ensures that you stay connected with your favorite music and radio programs.

## Features

- **Online Streaming:** Play songs from various online sites (currently one only).
- **Live Song Downloads:** Download live songs directly to your device.
- **All India Radio Stations:** Access and play a wide range of radio stations across India.
- **User-Friendly Interface:** Simple and intuitive design for easy navigation.

## Usage

1. Launch the app.
2. Navigate through the menu to choose between live AIR streaming, live song downloads, or online radio stations.
3. Enjoy your favorite music and radio programs!
